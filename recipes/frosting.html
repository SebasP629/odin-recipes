Frosting

Description

A vegan frosting with allergens that depend on your choice of milk and butter.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2-4 tablespoons milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Steps

  1. Add vegan butter to a medium bowl and beat with an electric mixer for a minute or two.
  2. Working 1 cup at a time, sift in the powdered sugar and mix until combined.
  3. Add in 2 tablespoons of almond milk and the vanilla extract. Mix until just incorporated, adding more almond milk if itâ€™s a bit too thick.
  4. Store in the fridge in an airtight container until ready to use.
