Snickerdoodle Cookies

Description

An allergy friendly recipe list: no eggs at all. The butter can be easily substituted for a vegan one. Gluten and wheat are still present, though.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup salted butter 1 stick
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/4 cup sugar (for the sugar coating)
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon (for the sugar coating)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Add flour, baking powder and cinnamon to a bowl. Stir and set aside.
  3. In another bowl, add sugar, brown sugar and room temperature butter. Beat with a mixer for about two minutes, until smooth.
  4. Add vanilla, water and oil to butter and sugar mixture and beat until combined.
  5. While mixing, slowly add in dry ingredients to wet ingredients until smooth.
  6. To make topping, combine sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl.
  7. Make dough into 1 inch balls and roll in sugar and cinnamon mixture until coated.
  8. Place 12 at a time on a baking sheet and bake for 9-11 minutes.
